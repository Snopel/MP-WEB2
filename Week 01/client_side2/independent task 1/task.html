Diploma of Business

Suitable for those with experience and skills in a variety of business functions but lacking
a formal education, you will acquire skills in finance, IT, human resources, marketing,
administration and project management.

Certificate II in Computer Assembly and Repair

Provides assembly and repair skills for computer hardware and operating systems. Introduces basic
electrical/electronic concepts. Learn the functionality of hardware and software components
and best practice in maintenance and safety issues. Completion gives you skills for the industry
recognised CompTIA A+ Core Hardware Examination. The Certificate II in Computer Assembly and Repair
is completed in conjunction with the Certificate II in Electronics to achieve a dual qualification.

Certificate IV in Computer Systems Technology

This new qualification combines the key elements of information technology and computer systems
to provide the skills and knowledge to install and administer simple networks, servers and client
desktop deployment. Learn how to apply programming concepts, project management, web design and
client needs analysis to the development of IT solutions in a business environment. Working in
industry with this qualification will allow you to apply a wide range of skills over a computing
and information technology areas especially in ICT support, database development, programming
and web development support.

Advanced Diploma of Computer Systems Technology

This is a new qualification that combines the key elements of the Information Technology
and Computer Systems skill areas to form an exciting new course. It provides a base core of
skill sets for an individual to coordinate and administer the installation and ongoing maintenance
of networks as well as the ability to develop programming and web development skills to undertake
application development activities. The first skill specialisation stream concentrates on the
design, testing and building of networks. The second skill specialisation stream concentrates on
the development of websites, designing of databases and the development of mobile application programs.

Certificate IV in Digital and Interactive Games

The Certificate IV in Digital and Interactive Games offers a creative and practical environment
to design and develop interactive digital works. Study areas include: concept art for human
computer interaction, 2D and 3D imaging and animation, sound, motion graphics and special effects,
video and manipulation, game design and development, web and app design and development.
